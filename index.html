About me

Hi, that's me, Yi Hao. Here's what I have been up to:
After completing a double degree engineering programme at the National University of Singapore and CentraleSupélec, Paris Saclay (formerly École Centrale Paris), I worked as a Marie Curie fellow under the ANNULIGhT Marie Sklodowska Curie ITN European project at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU). In collaboration with SAFRAN Helicopter Engines, I have been involved in the experimental investigation of ignition and lean blow-off phenomena of laboratory-scale annular combustors. Exploring the use of non-carbon fuel, such as ammonia and hydrogen, has also been part of my work.

I am currently open to job opportunities in Norway, France, Singapore or where exciting opportunity abound!
