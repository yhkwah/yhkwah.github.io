Lab Safety

Safety first! as always. Many things can possibly go wrong in the laboratory. It is hoped that with these checklists, (newbie) users in the Combustion Lab of NTNU will be able to safely navigate through the many stressful experimental campaigns with all limbs and equipment intact. The first checklist is for general use, and the second is relevant if one is using ammonia gas. Thanks everyone for their inputs and contributions! Much appreciated.
